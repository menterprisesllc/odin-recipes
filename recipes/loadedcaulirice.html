Loaded Cauliflower Rice

Description

This dish is a great alternative to loaded bake potatoes. You love the familiar flavors but with a healthier base. There's no skipping good taste with this healhier alternative.

Ingredients

  • cauliflower rice
  • onions
  • mushrooms
  • green peppers
  • jalapeno peppers
  • cream cheese
  • cheddar cheese
  • avocado ranch
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. First sautee the onions, green peppers, and mushrooms until caramelized
  2. Next add the cauliflower rice with the sauteed veggies
  3. Once the cauliflower rice has cooked for about 6 to 8 minutes, add 2 oz of cream cheese
  4. Once plated, add the avocado ranch on top of the rice
  5. Then add the cheese, jalapeno peppers, and top with sour cream
